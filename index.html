In Brazil, Germany, Russia, and many other countries around the world, the “okay” sign is a very offensive gesture because it is used to depict a private bodily orifice. The sign actually does mean “okay” in the United States, however in places like Japan it means “money,” and it is commonly used to signify “zero” in France. This gesture isn't offensive wherever you go, so make sure you're aware of what the sign means depending where you are in the world.
